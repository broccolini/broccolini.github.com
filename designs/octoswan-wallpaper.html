After making the GitHub "black swan" posters I turned 2 of the designs into wallpaper for desktop and devices.

I made variations of the cream one into a faint grey background after a friend said he liked low contrast backgrounds. I then experimented and made myself a bright red version. I expected to find it too much, but I find the bright red color energetic and it helps make me feel motivated. I've totally fallen in love with it and have it on my iPhone too.

You can download all of the backgrounds I've made so far from my background downloads repo on GitHub

I'm working on a few other backgrounds and I am working on a site to host them all so that's in the pipeline.
