After seeing IronCat way back in April this year I couldn't help but think what octocat I would make. I'd recently watched Tron and so naturally I had to make OctoTron.

I decided to go with a version inspired by the first movie. It took me a while to finish with the small event of moving countries. But finally, here's OctoTron, now also proudly living on the octodex.

You can also grab him from my backgrounds repo for iPhone, iPad and desktop in a few variations. Enjoy.
